About CADRE Academy

CADRE Academy is a resource provided by the University of Virigina to help students, faculty and staff locate, register, and attend technology workshops. This site was conceived by CADRE and developed by SOMRC. We launched in January of 2018.

About CADRE

CADRE is a University-wide exchange designed to simplify the process of finding local research technology solutions. The CADRE website provides a comprehensive list of the software, services, training opportunities, storage solutions, and computational hardware available to UVA scholars. CADRE is multi-disciplinary and supports all eleven schools of the University.

CADRE includes the following groups and departments:

  • Vice President for Information and Technology
  • School of Medicine - Research Computing
  • UVA Library - Research Data Services
  • Data Science Institute
  • ITS - Research Computing Infrastructure
  • UVA BioConnector
  • Advanced Research Computing Services
