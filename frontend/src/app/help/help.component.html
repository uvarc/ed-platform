CADRE Academy helps students, faculty and staff at the University of Virginia locate and register for free technology workshops hosted on campus. Anyone can view the site and look up workshops, but you will need a UVA id in order to register.

Using CADRE Academy

The two core parts of the CADRE Academy system are workshops and tracks, represented throughout the system by panels in two different styles. Workshop panels are smaller and represent any individual workshop that you can take through the CADRE Academy system. Track panels are larger and represent a set of workshops in a logical group or curriculum.

Workshops

Workshop panels provide the name of the workshop, the professor teaching it, the next available session of the workshop, and the workshop designation. An icon is added to the workshop panel to indicate which workshops you have registered for and which workshops you have completed.

Tracks

Tracks use a similar set of marks to help you determine your progress through the track. The overall layout of the track indicates the workshops you should take, and in what order (where applicable), while the individual marks let you know which workshops you have already registered for or completed.
